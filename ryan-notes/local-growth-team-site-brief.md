# Local Growth Team Website Brief

## Purpose

Build a simple static HTML/CSS website that presents our two-person growth consulting team as a credible, practical solution for local owner-operated businesses.

The site is not meant to look like a generic agency. It should feel local, direct, useful, and grounded. The goal is to help us explain the business idea clearly and make the team value obvious.

## Working Positioning

We help local businesses find and fix the biggest leaks in their digital revenue system.

We are not a traditional agency, a web design shop, or a long-term retainer vendor. We work in short, focused projects that diagnose problems, fix what matters, and leave the business better than we found it.

## Core Team Story

This business works because the two partners have complementary strengths.

Partner 1 is a digital growth strategist with expertise in:
- CRO
- Paid media
- Creative strategy
- Customer acquisition
- Digital campaign performance
- Finding business and marketing problems

Partner 2 is a technical architect with expertise in:
- Distributed systems
- Troubleshooting
- Automation
- Integrations
- Website systems
- Technical implementation
- Solving hard technical problems

The simple internal framing:

She finds the opportunities.
He removes the constraints.

Or:

She finds the leaks.
He fixes the pipes.

## Tone

The tone should be:
- Clear
- Local
- practical
- confident
- friendly
- non-corporate
- outcome-focused

Avoid:
- Generic agency language
- startup buzzwords
- vague “digital transformation” claims
- sounding like SaaS
- sounding like a marketing retainer agency

## Visual Direction

Create a clean, modern, mostly text-driven one-page site.

Suggested style:
- Warm local-business feel
- Editorial layout
- Big confident headline
- Simple cards for services
- Strong contrast
- No stock-photo-heavy agency aesthetic
- Optional subtle references to neighborhood/community without being cheesy

Use plain HTML and CSS only. No build tools required.
